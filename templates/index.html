Five steps to backup Instagram to Flickr.

  1. Create a recipe on IFTT to instanty copy photos from Instagram to Flickr in it's original 612x612 resolution.
  2. When you want to backup original sized photos to Flickr, upload them to Dropbox and share a folder.
  3. Login with your Flickr credentials
  4. Give us a share folder at Dropbox
  5. Press Analyze and wait for a 10 minutes (depends on a number of photos)
  6. Check photos you want to replace with it's originals and press "Replace"
